Akan names are of a Ghanian origin.

Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name based on their gender as well as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

This application will take your date birth and compute which day of the week you were born then assign you the appropriate Akan Name! How cool is that?!
